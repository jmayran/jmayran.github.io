My Favorite Spots


Bell Mountain

My favorite spot to be is on top of Bell Mountain. The view is breathtaking and it really makes you feel at peace.



Atlanta

I enjoy all the sites and restaurants in Atlanta, as well as, discovering new places to enjoy.



Sanford Stadium

This is the home of the Georgia Bulldogs football team, and my favorite place to watch a football game.



The Nook

The Nook is my favorite restaurant in Atlanta, and has the best stuffed burgers in town.



Turner Filed

Turner Filed is the home of the Atlanta Braves. I first went to Turner Field during the 1996 Olympics. To this day I still enjoy going to watch my favorite baseball team play.
